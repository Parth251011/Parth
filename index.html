WHY PREVIOUS PAPERS?

Solving previous year question papers can significantly improve your
exam performance. Firstly, it familiarizes you with the exam pattern
and question types, reducing surprises on the actual exam day.
Secondly, it helps you identify important topics, allowing you to
prioritize your study areas effectively. Lastly, it improves your time management skills,enabling you to complete the exam within the
allotted time while enhancing your speed and accuracy.

Additionally, practicing with past papers serves as an excellent
self-assessment tool. It lets you gauge your preparation level,
bridentify strengths and weaknesses, and adjust your study
plan accordingly. This self-assessment is crucial for effective
revision and helps reduce exam anxiety by building confidence
and familiarity with the exam format.

Finally, solving previous year question papers enhances your answer
writing skills. It teaches you to present concise and relevant answers
while improving problem-solving skills under real exam conditions.
By consistently practicing with these papers, you can track your
progress, monitor improvement, and boost your overall exam readiness.
